ACME CLEANTECH SOLUTIONS — ANNUAL REPORT 2024

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------

and carbon credit management software for mid-market industrial companies.
Founded in 2019, headquartered in Austin, TX.

RISKS AND DISCLOSURES
----------------------
1. Customer Concentration: Top 5 customers represent 28.7% of ARR.
   Loss of National Grid Partners alone would reduce ARR by ~8%.

2. Regulatory Dependency: 40% of carbon credit management revenue
   is directly tied to voluntary carbon market standards (Verra VCS,
   Gold Standard). Recent SEC scrutiny of carbon credit accounting
   creates policy uncertainty.

3. Competitive Landscape: Microsoft (Sustainability Cloud), Salesforce
   (Net Zero Cloud), and SAP (Green Ledger) have all entered the market
   in 2023–2024 with significantly larger go-to-market resources.

4. Churn: 3 enterprise customers churned in Q4 2024 (combined $680K ARR),
   citing budget constraints and preference for incumbent ERP vendors.

5. Key Person Risk: CTO Maria Chen (founding engineer, 6 patents) has
   received competing offers; retention package under negotiation.
